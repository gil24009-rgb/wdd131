Chocolate Chip Cookies

30 min • 24 cookies

⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ☆

Soft, chewy chocolate chip cookies with crisp edges and lots of melty chocolate chips—perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars together, then add eggs.
  3. Stir in dry ingredients and fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop onto a baking sheet and bake until golden around the edges.
